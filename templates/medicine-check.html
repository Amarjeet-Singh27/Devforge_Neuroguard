How It Works

Hash Chain Technology

Every medicine batch is tracked using SHA256 hash chains, creating an immutable record of its journey through the supply chain.

Supply Chain Tracking

From manufacturer -> distribution -> pharmacy -> patient. Each step is cryptographically verified.

Authenticity Verification

No tampering detected means the medicine traveled through the legitimate supply chain without any modifications.

Safe Indicator:

If you see "Authentic", the medicine is genuine and has not been tampered with.
